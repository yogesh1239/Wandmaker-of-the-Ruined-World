## English Localization

### 05 — Dragon-Furnace-Carved Seven-Layer Blue Wand Cyanos — continued

`...but dragons are better known to the general public as monsters with this kind of multilayered structure. Dragons are powerful monsters, and their large Gremlins possess natural multilayered structures that amplify magical power.`

### 06 — Life with a Witch

`A wand craftsman who knows nothing about magic is a little strange. Would you want to visit a ramen shop whose cook had never eaten ramen? Learn magic, and start making products tailored to magic-wand users.`

### 07 — Tokyo Witches' Council

`The void left by the Bloodsucking Mage is enormous. Without its central pillar, the Witches' Council is falling apart. Its members distrust one another, force their own positions on others, and cannot negotiate or trade smoothly. Many witches do not attend the meetings at all. Amid all that, the Blue Witch and Foresight Mage reached a useful agreement, and the Witches' Council fulfilled its purpose for the first time in ages. The Eyeball Witch was moved. Her relentless efforts to hold together a council on the verge of breaking apart had paid off.`

### 08 — Magic Linguistics

`Stoats are cute. That is the only thing you need to remember today. You may go home now.`

### 09 — Tokyo Magic University

`The Foresight Mage is one of the story's most overburdened characters. He ended up carrying Tokyo's future on his shoulders because he happened to acquire overwhelmingly powerful magic far beyond his own capacity—and because he chose to use it for the public. Despite being brutally overworked, he asks for very little. Just make a fuss over him. Insults hurt his feelings. Be kind.`

### 10 — Magic-Excitation Acoustic Appraisal

`A craftsman must have a discerning eye. What kind of chef cannot judge the quality of ingredients? What kind of carpenter cannot tell good timber from bad? Anyone aiming to become first-rate should learn to recognize first-rate materials.`

### 11 — The Dragon Witch

`This is the story where the great Dragon-sama plays a magnificent role. If you have time to read this, you should be offering me as much treasure as you can. Hiding it won't do you any good.
