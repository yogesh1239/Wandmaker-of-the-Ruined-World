The azure magic stone in its core has a notorious history. It originally belonged to the Edogawa Witch and was lent to members of the Tokyo Witches' Council as needed, seeing action in many battles. Its theft by the Iruma Mage triggered the coup, and after the Blue Witch killed him, it ultimately became hers. Since the stone was discovered, more than eight hundred people have shed blood over it.
