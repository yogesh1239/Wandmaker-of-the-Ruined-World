## English Localization

`Her real name is Aoyama Hiyori. She treasures her family but has no interest in anyone else. She was originally a beautiful high-school model raised in a wealthy family. She had a handsome, intelligent, athletic, kind, multitalented childhood friend; a father employed by a leading corporation; a beautiful mother; and an adorable younger sister. Her friendships and family relationships were harmonious. Her modeling career was a huge success. She was cheerful and lively, with kindness and cuteness beneath her cool exterior, and popular wherever she went. Aoyama Hiyori had everything. Immediately after the Gremlin Disaster turned her into a witch, the Witch of Ome was afraid to fight. She had lived a peaceful life untouched by violence, so fighting terrified her. At first, while Hiyori cowered, her male childhood friend led a group of local male volunteers and courageously fought the monsters in her place. Once Hiyori found the courage to fight, a community formed throughout the surrounding area with the “Witch of Ome” at its center. The Witch of Ome's father managed food distribution, her mother handled personnel matters, and her childhood friend organized combat support for her, such as sentry duty and reports of monster sightings. Thanks in part to that support, things went well at first. But as the Self-Defense Forces and police were wiped out, food supplies dwindled, and hope for the future faded, the atmosphere grew ominous. Her father was the first to break under the stress. He used his control over food distribution as leverage to threaten young women and prey upon them. He victimized far too many people in a short time, and some took their own lives. By the time the Witch of Ome discovered her father's crimes, the damage could no longer be undone, and she was the only person who could stop him. Words failed to persuade him. He had fallen too far. After many twists and turns, the Witch of Ome had no choice but to kill her father. The shock of her daughter killing her husband caused her mother to break under the stress as well. She lost interest in her family and began avoiding them. A handsome young villain whispered sweet words in her ear and seduced her into a secret intimate relationship. At his bidding, she sold the residents of Ome to criminals one after another as slaves, officially calling it relocation to other districts. By the time the Witch of Ome recovered from the shock of killing her father and could look beyond herself again, it was already too late. She had no choice but to kill her mother when the woman tried to sell even her younger sister. While the Witch of Ome secluded herself in her room after killing both parents with her own hands, her childhood friend came to see her. He had narrowly escaped death again and again, and the unrelenting stress had driven him mad. He had gradually grown overbearing and begun using force to satisfy his desires. As the Witch of Ome lay curled up and crying on her bed, her childhood friend tried to sexually assault her. She killed him too. By then, disturbing rumors had begun circulating among Ome's residents. Whatever the reasons, the Witch of Ome was killing those closest to her one after another. More than a few people questioned her sanity. Yet far more sympathized with, supported, and trusted her. Her younger sister never doubted her and believed in her with all her heart. Then that sister died of illness. Her grave stood behind the house. The Witch of Ome felt as though her heart would tear apart. Even so, for the sake of her fellow members of the Witches' Council, and for the many anxious residents of Ome who still believed in her as one central figure after another disappeared, she put on a brave face and held firm. Everything`
