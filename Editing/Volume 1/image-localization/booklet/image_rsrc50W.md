## English Localization

- Left quote: `“I'm just bad with people. It doesn't mean I hate them.”`
- Heading: `ORI KENSHI — OUTFIT VARIATIONS`
- Biography: `Born in Aichi Prefecture. After graduating from university, he moved to Tokyo and found a job, but his workplace relationships soured almost instantly when he adamantly refused to attend the new-employee welcome party. He quit in under ten days. After that, he saved money while drifting between part-time jobs, eventually renting a detached house in Okutama and making a living through online auctions. His family consists of his parents and older sister. They have cut ties with him (and he considers that reasonable). He likes anime, manga, movies, games, and other subcultures. He has what people call poor social skills. Dealing with people is so difficult for him that face-to-face meetings and conversations cause enough stress to make him physically ill. But people are merely difficult for him; he does not hate them. He wants them to live healthy lives somewhere that has nothing to do with him. He has no trouble maintaining relationships through online chat or correspondence. He considers his poor social skills a trait rather than a flaw. Because he is overly logical, he tries to understand interpersonal relationships in particular by fitting them into his own system of reasoning. For better or worse, he never lies. Every word out of his mouth is what he truly thinks, so people who dislike him hate him intensely, while people who like him like him just as intensely. Before the Gremlin Disaster, Ori had unfortunately never met anyone compatible with him.`
