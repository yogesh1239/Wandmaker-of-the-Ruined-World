## English Localization

### Matsuo Suetsugu

`An administrator who originally served the Bloodsucking Mage in Minato Ward. He graduated from a former imperial university and joined a major corporation, but struggled with personal relationships, and his life was not going well. Monster attacks unleashed by the Gremlin Disaster physically destroyed his company. He began working as an administrator for the Bloodsucking Mage, whose guidance mellowed his personality until the people around him came to think, “He's a little irritating, but he gets the job done, so whatever.” When he heard that the Bloodsucking Mage had died during the giant kaiju invasion, he initially refused to believe it and desperately searched the burned-out city for days. After going to work for the Foresight Mage, he continued to display his administrative ability to the fullest. However, his tendency to compare the Foresight Mage with the Bloodsucking Mage annoyed the staff of Bunkyo Ward Office. After the Foresight Mage's secretary delivered a tremendous slap and a furious lecture that left him traumatized, he settled down and began doing excellent work, including in his relationships with others. Since that lecture, he has been unable to stand up to young women.`

### Izumi Nagisa

`A woman who serves as the Foresight Mage's secretary at Bunkyo Ward Office. She had originally just joined the same company as the Foresight Mage, and they lived in neighboring rooms of the company housing. Soon after the Gremlin Disaster, the Foresight Mage rescued her from a monster attack, and she developed feelings for him. She rapidly developed her administrative ability with a focus on secretarial work, and she has now become indispensable to supporting the Foresight Mage's duties. She presents a demure face in front of him, but is actually rather strong-willed.`

### Kusunoki Sakura

`A girl born and raised in Okutama. She is an elementary-school student one grade below Ohinata Kei. She had been bedridden with influenza since the day of the Shantak Meteor Shower. Contact was lost with her father, who worked in central Tokyo. Her mother told Sakura not to leave the house and went to find him, but never returned. Even after her symptoms subsided, Sakura obeyed and never went outside. She survived on instant food while waiting for her mother. When the household food finally ran out and she timidly ventured outside, the people of Okutama were already gone. A randoseru is a sturdy backpack built to withstand six years of rough use by an elementary-school student, which made it useful for carrying supplies. A monster once attacked Sakura from behind, but the randoseru saved her life. By the time she met Ori, she was worn down to skin and bone. Her injuries barely healed, and sheer willpower kept her moving. After accepting Ori's parting gift, she left Okutama to search for her mother. She nearly entered Oume City, but changed course after seeing a warning sign that read, “Unauthorized intruders beyond this point will be killed.” She wandered into the Dragon Witch's territory, where she was taken in and protected. Assigned to the Food Team, she worked in the fields. The new crop seeds she brought with her were highly valued.`
