- `DIAMETER: 20 mm`
- `COLOR: MILKY WHITE`
- `LENGTH: 30 cm`
- Description: `The second magic wand made by Wand Maker Ori Kenshi. Built as a practical everyday combat wand, it has a short handle for ease of use. Its core is a milky-white Gremlin collected at Okutama Substation. Ori named the wand after the substation.
