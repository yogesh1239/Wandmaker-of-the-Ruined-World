## English Localization

- Heading: `OHINATA KEI — CASUAL OUTFIT VARIATIONS`
- Right quote: `“I'll finish my father's research, even if it costs me my life.”`
- Biography: `Ohinata Kei originally lived in Ome City. Her father, a linguistics professor at a certain university, gave her an elite education, and she consistently ranked at the top of her grade at her private elementary school. She had many friends and was well liked by her teachers. She was pure sunshine. Then her mother's affair came to light and her parents divorced. In exchange for giving the detached house in Ome City to her mother, they agreed that Kei would be raised by her father. Her mother remarried the man with whom she had been having the affair. Not long after Kei and her father moved to Minato Ward, the Gremlin Disaster struck. Her father formed a team under the Bloodsucking Mage, an old acquaintance, to study magic language. With her school closed and Kei feeling lonely, she spent nearly all her time in her father's laboratory. Her father kept his daughter away from the dangerous side of magic-language research. He made sure she would not witness the fatal accidents caused by failed experiments. Even so, Kei knew perfectly well that her father was risking his life on research meant to turn the calamity of the Gremlin Disaster around. Then the research team was wiped out. One member survived and was supposed to assemble a second research team, but a freak accident killed all the remaining survivors at once. Their hope was entrusted to Kei. With the Bloodsucking Mage as her guardian, she absorbed every scrap of material the research team had left behind. Just when the way had opened to resume magic-language research, the Bloodsucking Mage died. Kei was taken in by the Foresight Mage. She was determined to finish her father's research herself. To Kei, who had grown up watching her father's example and holding his hand, he had been her role model. She loved him more than anyone in the world. If he was gone, then at the very least she would accomplish what he had wanted to do. She loved linguistics and probably would have followed the same path even without what happened to her father, but his involvement transformed the depth of her resolve. And so Kei, who intended to risk her life to complete the fertility-magic bypass incantation, was introduced to the Blue Witch by the Foresight Mage.`
