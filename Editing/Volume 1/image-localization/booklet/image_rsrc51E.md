### 吸血の魔法使いの誓い

`吸血の魔法使いは様々な施策を打ち出していた。魔法言語研究、詠唱改造もその一つ。大日向（父）とは旧知で、危険な詠唱研究を依頼する代わりに、娘を必ず守り抜くと約束した。大日向（父）が集めた研究チーム全員にも同様の家族や恋人や友人を守る約束をしていた。研究チームは命と引き換えに魔法言語の研究を進めていった。その挺身に報いるためにも、吸血の魔法使いは決して大怪獣の前から退かなかった。吸血の魔法使いは約束を完璧に守り抜き、港区民を一人残らず逃がし、死んだ。`

### 吉田予想

`故・吉田准教授が提唱した発音不可能音に関する予想。吉田予想によれば、魔法言語には母音が6種類ある。á i ú é ó × の6種類である。子音、小音、長音と発音不可能母音が組み合わさる事で12種類の音ができる。①×　②小文字×　③長音×　④k×　⑤s×　⑥t×　⑦n×　⑧h×　⑨m×　⑩y×　⑪r×　⑫w×。以上で12種類。十二面フラクタルとは無関係で、12という数字は単なる偶然の一致。`

### 魔法言語の認識

`魔法言語を詠唱したり詠唱を聞いたりした場合、超越者は母国語の訳語を同時に認識できる。普通の人間が詠唱を聞いても、訳語が思い浮かんだりはしない。`

### ドラゴンの財宝嗅覚

`ドラゴンは財宝を嗅ぎ分け、探知する特有の嗅覚を持つ。大きなドラゴンであるほど、この能力は研ぎ澄まされる。ただし都心部ではあまり役立たない。街中が財宝（ドラゴンが財宝と認識するもの）で溢れ返っているため「そこらじゅう濃い匂いだらけで、鼻が利かない」状態になってしまうのだ。`

## English Localization

### Using the Legacy of Civilization — continued

### Magical Girl Logical☆Einstein

`An anime that once aired on television. Ori, the Blue Witch's younger sister, and the Northern Witch were fans. Ori was bitter after losing a merchandise lottery. The Blue Witch's sister begged for expensive limited-edition goods for her birthday and was overjoyed when she received them. The Northern Witch immersed herself in the feeling that she had become one of the anime's magical girls.`

### The Blue Witch's Tattered Coat

`A cherished coat she chose together with her younger sister. It is high quality, but now in tatters. No matter how ragged it becomes, she keeps wearing it.`

### The Bloodsucking Mage's Oath

`The Bloodsucking Mage launched many initiatives. Magic-language research and incantation modification were among them. He was an old acquaintance of Ohinata's father. In exchange for asking him to conduct dangerous incantation research, the Bloodsucking Mage promised to protect his daughter at all costs. He made the same promise to every member of the research team assembled by Ohinata's father: he would protect their families, lovers, and friends. The research team advanced the study of magic language at the cost of their lives. To repay their sacrifice, the Bloodsucking Mage refused to retreat before the giant kaiju. He kept his promise perfectly, got every last resident of Minato Ward to safety, and died.`

### Yoshida Conjecture

`A conjecture concerning unpronounceable sounds proposed by the late Associate Professor Yoshida. According to the Yoshida Conjecture, magic language has six vowels: á, i, ú, é, ó, and ×. Combining consonants, small sounds, and long sounds with the unpronounceable vowel produces twelve sounds: 1. ×; 2. lowercase ×; 3. long ×; 4. k×; 5. s×; 6. t×; 7. n×; 8. h×; 9. m×; 10. y×; 11. r×; 12. w×. That makes twelve in total. This has nothing to do with the dodecahedral fractal; the shared number twelve is pure coincidence.`

### Recognition of Magic Language

`When Transcendents chant magic language or hear an incantation, they simultaneously recognize its translation into their native language. When an ordinary human hears an incantation, no translation appears in their mind.`

### A Dragon's Nose for Treasure

`Dragons possess a unique sense of smell that can distinguish and locate treasure. The larger the dragon, the sharper this ability becomes. It is not very useful in a city center, however. The streets overflow with treasure, or things a dragon recognizes as treasure, leaving the dragon in a state where “strong scents are everywhere, so my nose doesn't work.”`
